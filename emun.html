Conference Details

It is with immense pleasure and honor that we extend our warm invitation to your esteemed institution to participate in the NUST EME Model United Nations (EME MUN), scheduled to take place from the 2nd to 4th of May 2024. The EME MUN conference serves as a platform for students to sharpen their diplomatic acumen and hone their public speaking skills while fostering a sense of global citizenship and responsibility. With five diverse committees including UNSC, SPECPOL, DISEC, SSFT, and PNA, participants will have the opportunity to engage in nuanced debates on pressing global issues, exploring multifaceted solutions and perspectives. NUST EME, renowned for its academic excellence and commitment to holistic development, is proud to host the EME MUN. We are dedicated to providing an enriching and organized experience for all participants, ensuring an intimate and impactful conference. We believe that your institution's participation will not only enrich the discourse but also contribute to the success of the EME MUN. Your presence and active engagement will be invaluable in fostering meaningful dialogue and advancing the cause of international cooperation and diplomacy. Included herein are further details, for your reference. Your presence will be appreciated. We look forward to hearing from you.

  • Regular Registration: Begins after 19th April, 2024
